Privacy Policy — Viral Machine

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Viral Machine collects only the minimum information necessary to provide its services. This includes TikTok OAuth access tokens required to post content to your account on your behalf.

2. How We Use Your Information

Access tokens are used solely to post video content to your TikTok account as directed by you. We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

3. TikTok Data

We access your TikTok account only to post content on your behalf using the Content Posting API. We do not store your TikTok credentials or access any TikTok data beyond what is necessary for posting.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Access tokens are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
